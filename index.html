BIOGRAPHY

Vermingod first spawned their death metal nature in 2003
gaining notable attention and therefore success within the local underground scene.
2010 saw the release of their debut album 'The Grand March to Devastation'.
2012 'Symptom Cult' EP.
2015 second LP 'Whisperer of the Abysmal Wisdom'.

OFFICIAL MERCHANDISE

VERMINGOD: 20 YEARS OF RELENTLESS DEATH METAL


In 20 years of existence, Vermingod released 3 studio albums and performed hundreds of shows. Until this day they continue with the same passion and drive, to release their 4th studio album called "The Anti-Dogma Supremacy", an album that expels sheer brutality and diverse composition.
